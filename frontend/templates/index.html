Synthesis Rating + Composite Score
Current Ratio (Liquidity)
Current Assets / Current Liabilities

The Current Ratio measures a company's ability to cover its short-term obligations (due within one year) with its short-term assets.

  • ≥ 1.5 (Attractive): Indicates a solid short-term liquidity buffer.
  • 1.0 to 1.5 (Neutral): Standard baseline liquidity profile.
  • < 1.0 (Caution): Potential liquidity stress under financial distress.
yfinance + AV Fallback
Debt to Equity (Leverage)
Total Debt / Total Shareholder Equity

This ratio measures financial leverage. It reveals the proportion of equity and debt the company uses to finance its assets, indicating long-term solvency.

  • < 100%: Conservative capital structure with low risk.
  • 100% to 200%: Moderate leverage typical in stable sectors.
  • > 200% (Caution): Aggressive debt levels, representing elevated risk.
yfinance + AV Fallback
Return on Equity (ROE)
Net Income / Shareholder Equity

Return on Equity measures profitability efficiency by showing how much profit a company generates with the capital invested by its shareholders.

  • > 15% (Attractive): High capital efficiency and strong returns.
  • 0% to 15%: Standard profitability profile.
  • < 0% (Caution): Unprofitable operations eroding shareholder value.
yfinance + AV Fallback
Gross Margin (Pricing Power)
Gross Profit / Revenue

Represents the percentage of revenue retained after incurring direct manufacturing/delivery costs, signaling competitive moat and pricing power.

  • > 50% (Strong): Exceptional pricing power and competitive moat.
  • 25% to 50% (Moderate): Balanced pricing spreads.
  • < 25% (Thin): Low competitive buffer or commodity dynamics.
yfinance + AV Fallback
Momentum Percentile Rank
Percentile Rank of Returns vs Peers & 14-Day RSI

Measures price returns over 6-month and 12-month periods, ranking them vs. the peer universe from 0 to 100. Also incorporates an independent 14-Day Relative Strength Index (RSI) sourced via Alpha Vantage.

  • ≥ 70th Percentile / RSI > 60: Favorable market leadership and momentum.
  • 35th-70th Pct / RSI 40-60: Standard mid-pack performer.
  • < 35th Percentile / RSI < 40: Market laggard showing weak demand.
yfinance + Alpha Vantage
Technical Metrics (step1c)
OHLCV → ATR, HV, Volume %, % Off 52W High, Acc/Dis≈

Computes 5 market-structure metrics from 2 years of daily price/volume data:

  • Volume % vs 50d Avg: (Last Vol − Avg50d) / Avg50d
  • % Off 52W High: (Price − High52W) / High52W
  • Hist. Volatility: Annualised 20-day stdev of log returns × √252
  • ATR (14-day): Avg of max(High−Low, |High−PrevClose|, |Low−PrevClose|)
  • Acc/Dis≈: Cumulative OBV proxy - independently estimated, not IBD's rating.
yfinance (2y OHLCV)
Earnings Quality - Sloan Accruals (step1d)
(Net Income − OCF) / Total Assets  |  OCF / Net Income

Based on Sloan (1996). Measures whether earnings are backed by real cash flows or non-cash accruals.

  • Accruals Ratio < 0: Cash flow exceeds reported income - high quality.
  • Accruals Ratio > 0.1: Significant non-cash accruals - lower quality signal.
  • Cash Conversion ≥ 1.0: Earnings are well-supported by operating cash flow.
yfinance Cash Flow
Valuation Multiples (step1e)
P/E = Price / EPS  |  P/S = MktCap / Revenue  |  EV/EBITDA

Standard valuation ratios benchmarked against same-sector peers in the current analysis batch.

  • Peer Median P/E: Median P/E of same-sector batch tickers only - not a broad market benchmark.
  • EV/EBITDA: Enterprise Value / EBITDA - useful for comparing capital structures.
  • P/S: Price / Sales - relevant for high-growth companies with low/negative earnings.
yfinance (batch-scoped)
Competitive Moat - Durability (step1f)
CoV (Gross Margin)  |  CoV (ROIC Proxy)  |  CoV (Revenue Growth)

CoV = Standard Deviation / Mean. Lower CoV = more consistent performance = stronger moat proxy.

  • Margin Stability: CoV of annual gross margin across available years.
  • ROIC Persistence: CoV of net income / total assets (ROIC proxy) across years.
  • Revenue Consistency: CoV of year-over-year revenue growth rates.
yfinance Annual Financials
News Sentiment (step1g)
DuckDuckGo Search → LLaMA Classifier

Fetches news snapshots via DuckDuckGo, feeds them to a lightweight LLM (`get_llm("light")`), and summarizes the general press sentiment consensus.

  • Positive: Favorable catalysts (earnings beat, new product).
  • Neutral: Balanced coverage or low-impact news.
  • Negative: Corporate issues (downgrades, legal risks).

Composite Score (step2)
40% Momentum + 35% EPS Growth + 25% Gross Margin → 1–99

A single blended score (1–99) computed deterministically before the LLM synthesis step.

  • Momentum (40%): Average of 6m and 12m percentile ranks (already 0–100).
  • EPS Growth (35%): Latest-quarter YoY EPS % change mapped to 0–100 (50% growth → 100).
  • Gross Margin (25%): Gross margin value (0.0–1.0) scaled to 0–100.
  • Missing components reduce the effective weight proportionally.
